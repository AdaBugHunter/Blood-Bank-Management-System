About Tanzeem-e-Amal

**Tanzeem-e-Amal Jhelum** was established in 1984 in the health sector, initially focusing on blood bank services for patients in **Jhelum and surrounding areas**. It has now become **the biggest blood bank in Jhelum**, serving for over **30 years**.

Location & Contact

The organization is located at **Opp. Maj. Akram Shaheed Park, Shandar Chowk, Jhelum, Pakistan**. Contact us at: **+92 333 5861115**

Our Mission

Tanzeem-e-Amal's goal has always been to provide **reliable blood donation services** and address the **healthcare needs** of the Jhelum community.

Our Services
